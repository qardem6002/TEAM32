Circuit Project

Our team worked on creating logical gates including OR, AND, NOT, and NAND gates. This project helped us understand the fundamentals of digital circuits and the practical implementation of logic gates using transistors.

Logic Gates

  • OR Gate: Ensures output is high if at least one input is high.
  • NOT Gate: Outputs the opposite signal of what it receives.
  • AND Gate: Ensures all inputs must be high for the output to also be high.
  • NAND Gate: Outputs a low signal only when all inputs are high.
